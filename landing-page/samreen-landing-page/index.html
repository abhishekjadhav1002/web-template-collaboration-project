ABOUT US

Oranges are popular due to their natural sweetness, the many different types available, and the diversity of uses. For example, a person can consume them in juices and marmalades, eat them whole, or use zested peel to add a tangy flavor to cakes and desserts.

This popular citrus fruit is particularly known for its vitamin C content. However, oranges contain a range of other plant compounds and antioxidants that may reduce inflammation and work against disease.

In this article, we look at the many health benefits of oranges, their nutritional profile, and how to include more in the diet.

VARIETIES

Valencia Oranges

This red, medium-sized fruit becomes fully ripe in mid-October. It is mostly grown in Shopian and Kulgam.

Tangerines

A small, rounded, very crispy and sweet fruit variety that ripens in mid-September.

Red Delicious

A very popular and widely cultivated variety of apple that ripens in mid-September. Its flesh is red white, grainy and juicy.

Navel Oranges

A large orange with red and green color. It tastes a bit sour but sweetens with time and is available by late October.

Cara Cara Oranges

A quickly perishable variety that ripens in early July. It is the oldest variety of apples cultivated in the valley and is mostly consumed domestically

Blood Oranges

A variety with comparatively longer shelf life, it is crispy, juicy and has thick greenish-white flesh which turns golden upon ripening. It is available till January.
